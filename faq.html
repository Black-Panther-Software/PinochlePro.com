Frequently Asked Questions

Where did these characters come from?

They were inspired by playing with my wife's grandparents Bob and Shirley. They were so fun, witty, and sarcastic that I decided to memorialize them in this game.

Why don't you support _______ feature?

I may not know about it. If you ask nicely, I may just add it! Please submit your idea through the feedback site.

Is there anything I can do to say thanks for the game?

Leaving a 5-star review on the App Store not only shows your appreciation but also helps us reach more players! Higher reviews lead allows me to focus on adding new features and improvements to the game.

Why do I keep getting similar hands? Why is it not more random?

Pinochle Pro models a robust shuffling algorithm designed to mimic real-life card shuffling, ensuring fairness and unpredictability. While streaks of similar hands may occur occasionally, it's essential to recognize the extremely low probability of any two hands being exactly alike due to the vast number of possible combinations inherent in a shuffled deck of cards. With 48 cards in a Pinochle deck and over 3 x 1066 possible permutations (that's 48 multiplied by 47, then by 46, and so on down to 1, known as 48 factorial), the chances of repeating a hand are infinitesimally small.
