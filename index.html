The Story Behind Pinochle Pro: A Tribute to Bob and Shirley

Years ago, amidst the whirlwind of becoming a new parent, I stumbled upon an unexpected journey that would eventually lead to the creation of Pinochle Pro, a mobile game that has brought more joy than I ever imagined. Today, I want to share with you the heartwarming story behind this game, inspired by two incredible individuals: Bob and Shirley Bearce.

It all began during visits to Sarah's grandparents, Bob and Shirley, at their retirement home in Mesa, Arizona. Whenever we gathered to play pinochle, it was a spectacle of laughter, bickering, and pure joy. Bob, in particular, had a knack for expressing his frustrations with the game in the most entertaining ways possible. His colorful commentary and Shirley's witty retorts filled the room with life and laughter.

Captivated by the charm of their banter, I started jotting down the funny remarks Bob would make during our games. Little did I know that these snippets of conversation would become the cornerstone of a project that would bring joy to countless people around the world.

Developing Pinochle Pro was a labor of love, crafted during nights and weekends while my kids were young. Now, with more free time ahead, I plan to invest further into the game. My goal is to add more family members, improve graphics, and make the characters more dynamic with better animations.

Pinochle Pro's Depiction of Bob & Shirley

In Pinochle Pro, players experience the fun of the game itself along with the dynamic interactions between animated characters. While they may not move much, their presence adds to the charm and nostalgia of the game.

Looking back, I am filled with gratitude for the inspiration that Bob and Shirley brought into my life. Their love for each other, their zest for living, and their infectious laughter continue to echo in the virtual world of Pinochle Pro. It is a reminder that even the simplest moments shared with loved ones can spark creativity and joy beyond measure.

Bob and Shirley (2009)

Bob and Shirley, unfortunately, passed away, but this game serves as a way to remember and honor them. Their spirit lives on in the joy and laughter that Pinochle Pro brings to players around the world.
